Our Story

At Alabash, we believe in the power of simplicity and quality. Our journey began with a passion for creating timeless pieces that effortlessly blend style and comfort. We are committed to sustainable practices and ethical production, ensuring that every garment is made with care and respect for both people and the planet. Our mission is to empower individuals to express their unique style through thoughtfully designed clothing that stands the test of time.

Our Values

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly materials and processes to minimize our environmental impact.

Ethical Practices

We ensure fair wages and safe working conditions for all our artisans and partners.

Community

We foster a supportive and inclusive community of customers and collaborators.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate the rich history of fashion by making authentic vintage pieces accessible to a new generation of rock enthusiasts. We believe that vintage clothing isn't just fashion - it's wearable history that connects us to the rebellious spirit and creative energy of rock's greatest eras.

Every piece we curate tells a story, and we're here to help you become part of that story.
